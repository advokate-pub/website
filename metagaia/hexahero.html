HexaHero is a tournament in the future where teams build robots to compete for fame, glory and cash prizes. In the year 2048, it has become the most popular spectator sport in the galaxy — drawing engineers, scientists and designers from all over the universe using their skills and wit. Each season, gamers compete for a grand jackpot prize with the chance to win other prizes and trophies along the way.

HexaHero battle mode pits two teams against each other. Each player selects a team of three Hexas and three Heroes to face off in each battle. Players take turns strategically playing cards to attack their opponent and defend their own team from damage. Each Hexa has different parts that are upgradable, and each Hero has different talents. Different combinations of Hexas and Heroes determine the stats for each HexaHero in battle.

Tournaments are run by season. At the end of each season, players with the highest scores are rewarded with cash prizes. Each season also introduces exclusive new NFT items that can only be earned by playing during that season.

Build up your HexaHero team and resources to win more tournaments!

Two Hexas can use "fusion" to produce a new Hexa with combined attributes. The new Hexa can be used in battle, fused with another Hexa, or sold in the marketplace. Each Hexa can only be fused a maximum of 6 times.

Players can purchase land in MetaGaia to build training dojos for Heroes and machine shops for Hexas. In the future, new gameplays are coming, like mining minerals, forging new weapons or Hexa parts, and building Hexa robots.

Explore the larger MetaGaia universe with your Hero! After you are done battling in the arena, unlock thrilling adventures with your Hero avatar. Coming in 2022!
