METG TOKEN

METG is the token used in our MetaGaia community. METG can be earned by staking tokens, playing games, and participating in key governance votes.
Payment
METG is our native token. Required to participate in certain sales and auctions in MetaGaia.
Governance
Soon after our token launch, our Reward Pool will start to receive revenues generated by MetaGaia as well as a portion of staking rewards. The Reward Pool will be governed by METG DAO.
Staking
Gamers can earn newly minted METG by staking. Minimum staking thresholds sometimes are required in order to vote and or receive various benefits and rewards in MetaGaia.
METG will be available on partner's exchanges.
